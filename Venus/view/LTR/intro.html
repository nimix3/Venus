About Venus Framework

We found out that we need a robust, easy, rapid and complete PHP framework to build websites and web applications with minimum requirements in less time. We are eager to build a lot of things, but we are human beings and the time is limited and our creativity and ideas can be wasted. That's because we create Venus Framework. we want that every developer can create his own ideas with spending less time and energy.
